Online yoga: don’t let anything stop you

Just 30 minutes a day of yoga offers a number of important benefits:

  • Increased Energy
  • Makes The Mind Clearer
  • Helpful in Reducing Body Pain
  • Helps Detoxify The Body
  • Aids In Improving Your Mood

6 benefits of online yoga classes

  1. Convenient

    You can join a class in real-time, or take a pre-recorded session whenever it works for you. Midnight yoga, anyone?

  2. Less expensive

    Since we don't have to accommodate a large physical space we can provide less expensive classes,or even free classes depending on your level and your schedules.

  3. Builds a home wellness practice

    You’ll build confidence moving through poses on your own.

  4. Adjusts to your pace

    Take a break, pause and come back whenever you need, or switch to a video that’s a little less intense

  5. Less intimidating

    Learn yoga in the safety and relative anonymity of your living room. You can turn your camera off or take an early savasana without fear of judgment. You can also be part of an amazing community we created.

  6. Practice anywhere in the world

    With a data connection, practice anywhere, anytime
